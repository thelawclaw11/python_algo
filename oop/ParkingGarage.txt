Specs:

Parking Garage will be initialized with a number of spaces
payment is based on size of car and duration of stay
user can ask if they can park their car
user is given a ticket with the amount they must pay

Use Case:

Actors:
    Parking Garage
    User

1. User pulls up to kiosk and asks if they can park their car
2. User is given ticket indicating the start time of their parking
3. User parks car
4. User leaves
5. User comes back and drives car to kiosk
6. User presents kiosk with ticket
7. Kiosk tells User how much money is owed
8. User pays kiosk

Extensions

1. there is no room for their car, they are told to leave
